Draconian announce new album ‘Under a Godless Veil’

Posted by Sudokof on June 5, 2020

Swedish gothy death metallers Draconian have announced their new album, ‘Under a Godless Veil,’ which marks the follow-up to their 2015 LP titled ‘Sovran,’ set for release on October 30, 2020 through Napalm Records.


The upcoming full-length delivers a captivating mélange of dreary doom and contrasting vocals and is already the second offer featuring the stunning singer Heike Langhans: The mix of Heike’s angelic, female voice and Anders Jacobsson’s rilling death growls will drag the listener into a sorrowful cosmos. Already the first notes of the opener will draw you into the void of interweaving beauty, coalescing with paralyzing depression that leaves nothing but the desire of total immersion in the whole record. Under A Godless Veil carries the legacy of gothic doom metal à la My Dying Bride into a new decade of the genre.
